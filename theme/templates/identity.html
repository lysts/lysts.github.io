About me.

Originally a pupil of neuroscience, I am a full-stack software developer, passionate about creating thought-evocative, interactive, and informative digital tools for any purpose,
Rather than letting technology engulf me, I have strived to develop a stable relationship with technology, by seeing its positive impact as science communication or online learning tools during the pandemic era.

I am also an audiophile, with a violin at hand wherever I go.
Insects are my favourite animals and I find great joy in capturing them in art.

I am also an avid walker, to bump into scenic natural landscapes and urban shapes
—— free art inspiration.

Hailing from Japan, I was born in the concrete jungle, Tokyo, on a street adorned with a thick veil of sakura trees.
Now, I find myself on the other side of the planet, in North London.

This internet garden.

This website is a reflection of my learning and a place to connect with others (say hi! email here).
*fun fact*, the website logo is a mixture of my initials embodying a waveform which represents my ties with my island roots, Hawaii and Japan.
